For economy reasons, the Newsletter is provided on-line at: http://www.lnhb.fr/conferences-publications/icrm-newsletter/. All laboratories engaged in radionuclide metrology are invited to contribute to the next issue. The Newsletter contributions are placed on-line with no alterations by the editor. To ensure readability and to avoid unnecessary work by the editor, a common simple format is used for all contributions. Contributions may be sent by e-mail as an attachment in MS Word or as a plain text file. A format document is available electronically from the Editor.
